Cancellation/Reschedule Policy

we provide a 20-minute grace period for your appointment. please note that if you do not show up within this window, you will need to reschedule. We encourage you to reschedule at least 48hours before your appointment if you are unable to attend. you are alllowed to transfer your initial deposit only once. if you reschedule twice, a new deposit will be required for the second appointment.

If you need to cancel your appointment, please do so atleast 48hours in advance. This will allow to fill the spot and accomodate other clients. we understand that unexpected can occur, making it neccesary to cancel your appointment. However cancellations without prior communication will result in a charge of 50% of the toal hair fee.

All appointments require a $2 deposit. This deposit secures appointment slot and will be applied towards your total service payment. Please note that deposits are non-refundable.
